Mix discard night before:

    4 oz. sourdough starter (can be your discard for the day)
    1 1/2 cups (12 oz.) buttermilk
    1 1/2 cups (195 g) all-purpose flour
    1 Tbsp. honey

In the morning add to the batter:

    2 large eggs
    4 Tbsp. melted & cooled butter
    1 tsp. baking soda
    1/2 tsp. salt
