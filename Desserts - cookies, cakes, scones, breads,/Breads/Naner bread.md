### Steps

1) Cream sugar and butter.
2) Add bananas and eggs.
3) Add flour, salt, and baking soda.
4) Optional: add chocolate chips, walnuts, pecans... 
5) Bake in greased loaf pan at 350F for 45-50 min.


### 1x naner bread

- 1/2-3/4 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 4 very ripe bananas
- 2 eggs
- 1 cups AP flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda

### 2x naner bread

- 1-1 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 6 very ripe bananas
- 4 eggs
- 2 cups AP flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp baking soda
